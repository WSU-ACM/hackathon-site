General Information

In order to make sure that you have all the time you need to prepare for the hackathon, we've prepared some informtaion for you.

Will there be food?

So much food. All meals will be covered as well as snacks along the way.

How many people can be on one team?

As of this year, you may have up to four students on one team. We've increased this number to allow for non-developer roles such as business-minded and engineering students. Note: the fourth person may be a developer if you wish. However, all members must be current high school or college students.
